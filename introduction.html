Profile

The Salvation Army is a Christian organisation in Malaysia and other country and their mission is both in spiritual and practical. Their social services are to provided people without looking at their background, race or religion.

This organization started their journey at Penang, 1938. The Salvation Army is become well known after upon a year because of their work inf residential care which is take care of children and elderly, help poor family also help for other people when natural disasters. The Salvation Army covers Peninsular Malaysia and East Malaysia, Sabah and Sarawak

Introduction

The Salvation Army come to Malaya (Malaysia) when Frederick Harvey made their first center in former YMCA building in Penang, in July 1938. Frederick Harvey run a free school for poor children. Upon a few years later, the Army manage to opened social programmes in other states like Ipoh, Melaka, Batang Melaka and Kuching.

Today

The Salvation Army in Malaysia has ten corps and other social service programmes which is provide spritual guidance and practical assistance to all the people especially people in need. The organization also developed other services to people who in need. For example home for elderly, day care centres, kindergartens and community service centres.
