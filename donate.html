Donation In-Kind

It is a great way to recycle and reuse things that are too good to throw away!

What can you donate?

The best things to donate are the things which you find are still in good condition but perhaps don't need or don't want to use anymore. As a simple rule; if there is something wrong with it, we cannot sell it. The typical things which we receive are:

  • Clothing
  • Furniture
  • Household items
  • Electronic devices
  • Toys
  • Books

Wills & Bequests

Some people's legacies are written on monuments; yours can be written on lives. The Salvation Army is ever dedicated to helping the needy.By including The Salvation Army in your Will, you can be assured that we can continue to help others for years to come.

  • Insurance

Whether it is life or any other type of policies, you can contribute a sum or policy towards The Salvation Army.

  • Wills

Distributing your estate through your Will is known as making a bequest. A will is a legal document, which can be drawn up by you or your lawyer to provide for the distribution of your assets.

Your bequest could go a long way in helping our beneficiaries see a better future.
